Our Story

Built from Legacy,
Powered by Wellness.

Hayti’s was inspired by the historic Hayti community of Durham, North Carolina — a thriving center of Black entrepreneurship, culture, and resilience.

Our mission is to nourish the body, uplift the mind, and restore the spirit through intentional wellness products rooted in culture, nature, and healing traditions.
